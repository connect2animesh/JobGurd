              How JobGuard Works

              Detection methodology explained

              🤖

              ML Classification

              A Logistic Regression model trained on labelled job postings predicts fake or real, outputting a confidence score.

              🔍

              Pattern Matching

              Over 60 regex patterns scan for fee requests, copy-paste work, urgency tactics, ad-posting, and MLM language.

              📧

              Email Domain Check

              Personal domains like Gmail or Yahoo are flagged. Legitimate companies use official company-owned emails.

              💰

              Salary Sanity Check

              Salaries too high per day or suspiciously below minimum wage are flagged using numeric parsing.

              🔗

              URL Validation

              Shortened links (bit.ly, tinyurl) and suspicious TLDs (.xyz, .tk) are flagged as high risk.

              📊

              Weighted Risk Score

              Each flag carries a weight. Final = 60% rules + 40% ML probability, capped at 100.

              ✅

              Positive Signals

              CIN numbers, PF/ESIC, offer letters, Glassdoor/LinkedIn links and background checks reduce the score.
